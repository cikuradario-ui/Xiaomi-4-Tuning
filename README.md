# Xiaomi Electric Scooter (Series 4) Tuning Tool

A powerful, user-friendly Windows executable (`.exe`) designed to tune, customize, and unlock the speed limits of your **Xiaomi Electric Scooter Series 4**. 

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
**Ride at your own risk.** Modifying your electric scooter's firmware may violate local traffic laws and can void your manufacturer warranty. Unlocking higher speeds can be dangerous and increases the risk of accidents. Always wear a helmet and protective gear. The developers take no responsibility for any damage to your scooter, fines, or injuries.

---

## Supported Models
This tool supports firmware tuning for the following Xiaomi 4-series electric scooters:
- 📱 Xiaomi Electric Scooter 4
- 📱 Xiaomi Electric Scooter 4 Lite
- 📱 Xiaomi Electric Scooter 4 Lite (2nd Gen)
- 📱 Xiaomi Electric Scooter 4 Pro
- 📱 Xiaomi Electric Scooter 4 Ultra

## Features
- **Speed Unlock:** Increase your maximum top speed beyond the factory limits.
- **Power Adjustment:** Customize motor torque and acceleration for a punchier start.
- **KERS Modification:** Adjust or disable the Kinetic Energy Recovery System.
- **Firmware Flashing:** Safe and optimized custom firmware flashing.

## How to Use

### Prerequisites
- A Windows PC or Laptop.
- bluetooth on PC or Laptop.
- Your Xiaomi Electric Scooter.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Download the executable:** Grab the latest release and execute.
2. **Turn on your scooter:** Make sure your scooter is powered on and Bluetooth is enabled on your device (if connecting wirelessly)#
4. **Connect:** Select your scooter model from the dropdown list and click **"Connect"**.
5. **Select your tuning:** Choose your desired speed limits and acceleration settings. 
6. **Flash:** Click **"Flash Firmware"** and wait for the process to finish. **Do not turn off your scooter or disconnect the cable during this process.**
7. **Reboot:** Once the flash is successful, turn your scooter off and back on again. Enjoy your ride!

---

## Troubleshooting
- **Connection issues:** Ensure your Bluetooth/cable connection is stable and that no other official Xiaomi apps are running in the background.
- **Flash failed:** Verify that your battery is sufficiently charged (above 50%) before attempting to flash firmware.
